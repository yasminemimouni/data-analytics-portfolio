# Business Intelligence – Vending Machine Sales & Payment Optimization
## Project Objective
Analyze vending machine sales data to identify consumption patterns, product performance, and payment preferences, and to support data-driven decisions on inventory management and machine optimization.

## Data & Tools
- Data: Monthly vending machine transaction data (January–August), anonymized
- Tools: Power BI, Excel
## Key Analyses & KPIs
- Global performance overview: Total sales, total quantity sold, and average selling price
- Time-based analysis: Monthly and weekly sales trends to identify peak demand periods
- Product performance: Best- and worst-selling products to optimize assortment
- Payment method analysis: Cash vs card vs mobile usage to support cashless strategy
- Category revenue breakdown: Revenue contribution by product category and brand

## Business Impact & Recommendations
- Identified peak sales periods and high-performing products to improve stock planning and reduce shortages
- Highlighted strong adoption of card and mobile payments, supporting investment in cashless-compatible machines
- Detected underperforming products and machines to support replacement and optimization decisions
- Provided actionable insights to improve operational efficiency and customer satisfaction
