## Dashboards Preview

### Financial Performance Dashboard
![Financial Performance](images/financial-dashboard.png)
Provides a detailed view of sales, purchases, paid vs unpaid invoices, margins, and VAT indicators, supporting financial monitoring and cash flow analysis.

### Products & Inventory Dashboard
![Products & Inventory](images/products-inventory.png)
Analyzes product profitability, margin performance, stock rotation, and inventory risk to support pricing and purchasing decisions.

### Customers & Suppliers Dashboard
![Customers & Suppliers](images/Customer Supplier.png)
Focuses on customer payment behavior, top customers by revenue, supplier performance, and geographic sales distribution to support financial risk management and commercial strategy.
