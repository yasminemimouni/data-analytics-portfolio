# Graduation Project (PFE) – End-to-End Business Intelligence & Predictive Analytics Platform
## Project Overview
Designed and implemented an end-to-end Business Intelligence and predictive analytics platform based on a centralized Data Warehouse. The solution provides multi-domain dashboards (Finance, Sales, Products, Clients, and HR) to support strategic, operational, and financial decision-making.

## Solution Architecture
- Data integration from multiple operational sources into a centralized Data Warehouse
- Analytical data modeling optimized for reporting and decision support
- Interactive Power BI dashboards for multi-level analysis
- Predictive analytics integrated to support forecasting and proactive decision-making
## Dashboard Structure & Business Focus

### 1. Executive Overview Dashboard
Provides a consolidated view of the company’s overall performance, including revenue, purchases, VAT indicators, customer distribution, and key HR metrics. Designed for top management to monitor global performance and strategic trends.

### 2. Financial Performance Dashboard
Focuses on detailed financial analysis, including sales and purchases, paid vs unpaid invoices, net VAT, and gross margin by product category and brand. Includes predictive indicators to anticipate future purchase volumes and spending.

### 3. Products, Margins & Inventory Dashboard
Analyzes product profitability, margins, stock rotation, and risk of stock shortages. Supports pricing decisions, inventory optimization, and purchasing strategy.

### 4. Customers & Suppliers Analysis Dashboard
Provides insights into customer payment behavior, top customers by revenue, geographic sales distribution, and supplier performance. Supports financial risk management and commercial strategy.

### 5. Human Resources Analytics Dashboard
Analyzes workforce structure, salary costs, recruitment sources, and employee satisfaction indicators. Includes salary forecasting to support workforce planning and HR decision-making.

## Tools & Technologies
- Data Integration & ETL: Talend, Python
- Data Storage & Modeling: Data Warehouse, SQL
- Data Visualization & BI: Power BI
- Predictive Analytics & Machine Learning: Python (forecasting models)
- Deployment & Integration: Flask, Angular
